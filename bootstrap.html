Panels

While not always necessary, sometimes you need to put your DOM in a box. For those situations, try the panel component.

By default, all the .panel does is apply some basic border and padding to contain some content.
This Panel is outside of the Container
Panel heading without title
Easily add a heading container to your panel with .panel-heading.
You may also include any <h1>-<h6> with a .panel-title class to add a pre-styled heading.
However, the font sizes of <h1>-<h6> are overridden by .panel-heading.

Progress Bars

Provide up-to-date feedback on the progress of a workflow or action with simple yet flexible progress bars.

Cross-browser compatibility Progress bars use CSS3 transitions and animations to achieve some of their effects. These features are not supported in Internet Explorer 9 and below or older versions of Firefox. Opera 12 does not support animations.
